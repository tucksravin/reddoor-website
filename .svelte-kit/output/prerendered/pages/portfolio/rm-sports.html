Brand, Print

RM Sports

We developed a brand, digital presentations and other identity collateral for RM Sports, a consulting agency for elite athletic teams.

RM Sports prides itself in "taking the guesswork out of high performance." In order to do so they focus on: training the trainers and coaching the coaches; providing supplemental expertise; measuring and facilitating competitive intelligence; augmenting performance development with the sport sciences; and teaching concrete techniques, skills and strategies.

Our challenge was to create a brand that felt at home in the competitive sports arena, that complemented the technical and detail-oriented consulting service, while speaking clearly to a wide audience. Our target demographic included NCAA Athletic Directors, NCAA coaches, all the way down to the athletes themselves.

Logo

The logo tells the story of how hunting for the smallest opportunities for improvement and efficiency can literally "change the game."
