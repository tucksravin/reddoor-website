Champion Auto Parts

A great collaboration with the creative team at Innocean USA, the Champion project began by updating packaging with a new design system that could be applied across a growing range of automotive and lifestyle products.

The objective was to create best-in-class packaging that would allow Champion to win back the faith of auto part retailers, and provide convincing reasons to stock the brand. "There's a Champion in Here" is a celebration of those who have an irrepressible drive to win at life.

At more than 100-years-old, Champion is a brand with legitimate heritage in motorsports. We took inspiration from that legacy in performance automotive culture and created a simple color scheme and a graphic illustration style that makes the brand immediately recognizable. We arrived at a contemporary look for the brand that is rooted in history and dependability.

The Champion Motor Speedway, M1 Concourse in Pontiac Michigan is an 87-acre playground for auto enthusiasts that includes 250+ Private Garages and a state-of-the-art 1.5-mile Performance Track. M1 is a place where auto enthusiasts can keep, show and exercise their vehicles in the company of other enthusiasts. M1 Concourse will become the epicenter of all things automotive in metro Detroit and provide the largest concentration of auto enthusiasts in the world a venue to enjoy their passion with friends and family.
