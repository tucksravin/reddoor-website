Texas Organ Sharing Alliance

The Texas Organ Sharing Alliance (TOSA) saves lives through the power of organ donation.

The TOSA logo has significant meaning and relates directly to the life-saving work they're committed to. The heart represents warmth, life and generosity. The heart shape blends into a partial infinity symbol, representing the continuation of life via organ donation. The infinity symbol alone can be read as the number eight, which is the number of lives a single organ donor can save. The blue-to-red color gradation represents how the loss of life brings about new life.
